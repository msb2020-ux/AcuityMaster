About the Transition to Cloud Licensing

AcuityMaster is transitioning from a legacy perpetual licensing model to a browser-based annual subscription (SaaS) effective July 1, 2026.

This change enables:

  • macOS and Windows compatibility (browser-based — no install)
  • Centralized license management for multi-seat practices
  • Automatic software updates — no manual installs
  • Elimination of hardware-locked licenses
  • Sustained ongoing development and long-term clinical reliability

Pricing is structured so that five years of subscription cost is roughly equivalent to the historical perpetual license price — ensuring fairness for long-standing customers.
