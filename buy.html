About the Transition to Cloud Licensing

AcuityMaster is transitioning from a legacy perpetual software licensing model to a browser-based annual subscription model (SaaS) effective July 1, 2026.

This change is required to:

  • Sustain ongoing software development and support
  • Enable macOS and Windows compatibility
  • Eliminate hardware-locked licenses
  • Provide centralized license management
  • Maintain long-term clinical reliability

Pricing has been intentionally structured so that five years of subscription cost is roughly equivalent to the historical perpetual license price, ensuring fairness to long-standing customers while positioning AcuityMaster for the future.
