About the Windows software

Our Windows-based digital acuity software is available for immediate purchase. Developed in a multispecialty ophthalmology and optometry setting, AcuityMaster lets you efficiently assess visual acuity and refract your patients with a precise, robust, and affordable solution.

If you're still checking visual acuity with a Snellen-only projector, AcuityMaster gives you a full computerized visual acuity system — including ETDRS, LogMAR, contrast sensitivity, Worth 4-dot test, color vision, and a wide range of optotypes and configurable settings that a simple projector simply can't provide.
