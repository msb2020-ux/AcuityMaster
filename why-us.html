Mac & Windows compatible

No device lock-in

Your license isn't tied to a single PC. Move to a new computer or add a display without re-purchasing or transferring a dongle.

Cost competitive

Perpetual pricing that is cost competitive with the leading alternatives over a 5-year horizon — without annual subscription fees for the Legacy edition.

Clinician-developed
