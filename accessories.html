Optional add-ons that make a real difference.

AcuityMaster works with a wide range of off-the-shelf accessories. None are required, but each can streamline how your team navigates the software during exams.

Up to three pedals can replace frequently used keystrokes (Shift, Ctrl, Alt, or any custom key/mouse function). About AcuityMaster

AcuityMaster is state-of-the-art digital visual acuity software — also referred to as a computerized chart projector, computer eye chart, computerized visual acuity system, or digital eye chart. It assesses contrast sensitivity, visual acuity via Snellen and pediatric-friendly optotypes, and runs on a dual-monitor or all-in-one computer mounted in any optometry or ophthalmology exam lane.
